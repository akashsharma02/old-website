DeepV2D: Video to Depth with Differentiable Structure from Motion

0. Abstract

  • Interleaves two stages: motion estimation and depth estimation. Both are alternated to converge to get depth and pose refinement

1. Introduction

  • Brute force methods generate camera motion first (SFM) and then generate dense depth from that
  • deep learning methods use texture gradients and shading, also differentiable modules can be composed. however, structural priors are necessary
  • Method is a composition of classical methods turned into differentiable network modules. interleaves camera motion optimization and stereo reconstruction. Can be imagined as block coordinate descent.
  • Flow-SE3 (their method) estimates dense 2D correspondences from pairs of frames, and then runs one iteration of GN to minimize reprojection error
  • DeepTAM only replaces depth prediction and feature matching with deep learning, while BA-Net tries to solve everything in one step, this method breaks it into two steps, as mentionedearlier
